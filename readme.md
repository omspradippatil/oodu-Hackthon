# 📌 Overview

PortOps AI is an intelligent transit and fleet management platform designed for **Vadhvan Port**, one of India's upcoming mega port developments.

The platform is built on the **TransitOps** problem statement and focuses on digitizing port transportation operations by synchronizing vehicles, operators, cranes, and container movement through a centralized ERP system.

The primary objective is to reduce operational delays, improve resource utilization, minimize turnaround time, and provide real-time visibility into port operations.

---

# 🚨 Problem Statement

Large ports handle thousands of container movements every day.

Managing these operations manually leads to:

- Vehicle congestion
- Delayed container movement
- Inefficient crane allocation
- Unplanned vehicle maintenance
- Poor driver scheduling
- Lack of real-time visibility
- Increased operational costs

As Vadhvan Port is expected to become one of India's major logistics hubs, efficient transport coordination becomes a critical requirement.

---

# 💡 Our Solution

**PortOps AI** acts as a centralized **Port Operations Command Center**.

Instead of manually coordinating vehicles and operators, the system intelligently manages and synchronizes:

- Container Transportation
- Fleet Allocation
- Driver Assignment
- Equipment Availability
- Maintenance Scheduling
- Fuel Monitoring
- Operational Analytics

The platform enables faster decision-making and efficient resource utilization across the port.

# 🌟 Unique Innovation

Unlike traditional fleet management systems, PortOps AI focuses on **Resource Synchronization**.

When a container movement request is created, the system coordinates available resources and assists managers in selecting the most suitable combination of:

- Vehicle
- Driver
- Equipment

This improves operational efficiency while reducing manual coordination.
