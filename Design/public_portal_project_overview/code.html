Project Overview

Vadhavan Port is a monumental greenfield deep-draft port development on the western coast of India, designed to handle mega-vessels and significantly enhance the nation's maritime trade capacity.

Strategic Location

Palghar District, Maharashtra

Key Infrastructure

directions_boat

Mega Berths

9 container terminals with 1000m quay length each, capable of accommodating 24,000 TEU capacity vessels. Features automated crane rails.

waves

Offshore Breakwater

A massive 10.14 km offshore breakwater structure ensuring calm basin conditions for year-round operational stability.

route

Hinterland Connectivity

Dedicated rail corridors connecting to the Western Dedicated Freight Corridor (DFC) and a multi-lane expressway link to the National Highway network.
